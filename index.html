--- layout: home title: Machine Learning for Energy and Material Science: From Theory to Industry Applications subtitle: Workshop @ ECML PKDD 2026 --- Workshop @ The European Conference on Machine Learning and Principles and Practice of Knowledge Discovery in Databases (ECML PKDD 2026)

Machine learning is transforming how we model, simulate, and optimise complex physical systems in energy, materials science, and climate. Governed by coupled, nonlinear dynamics and high-dimensional PDEs, these systems demand scalable, physically consistent solutions beyond the reach of classical methods.

This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners at the intersection of AI, physics, and engineering to advance physics-informed learning, neural operators, surrogate modelling, and multi-scale approaches — driving trustworthy, deployable AI for real-world scientific and societal impact.

Format: Half-day workshop with paper presentations and a keynote talk.
Audience: Researchers, engineers, and data scientists working on scientific ML, hybrid modelling, and multi-scale simulation in energy, materials, climate, and manufacturing.
